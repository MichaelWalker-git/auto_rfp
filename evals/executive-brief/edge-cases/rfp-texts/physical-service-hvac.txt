Title: Preventive Maintenance and Repair Services for HVAC Systems
Agency: Department of Veterans Affairs

PSC: J041 — Maintenance/Repair of Refrigeration, Air Conditioning Equipment

Posted: March 1, 2026

SET-ASIDE: Total Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) Set-Aside

1. SCOPE OF WORK

The VA Palo Alto Health Care System requires a qualified contractor to provide comprehensive preventive maintenance (PM) and repair services for approximately 450 HVAC units across three campus locations:

- Main Campus: 3801 Miranda Avenue, Palo Alto, CA 94304 (287 units)
- Menlo Park Division: 795 Willow Road, Menlo Park, CA 94025 (98 units)
- Livermore Division: 4951 Arroyo Road, Livermore, CA 94550 (65 units)

Equipment includes:
- Chiller systems (York, Trane, Carrier — 200-500 ton capacity)
- Air Handling Units (AHUs) including HEPA-filtered units for surgical suites
- Variable Air Volume (VAV) boxes
- Package units and split systems
- Cooling towers
- Building Automation System (BAS) interfaces — Johnson Controls Metasys

2. PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE
Base Year: October 1, 2026 — September 30, 2027
Option Year 1: October 1, 2027 — September 30, 2028
Option Year 2: October 1, 2028 — September 30, 2029
Option Year 3: October 1, 2029 — September 30, 2030
Option Year 4: October 1, 2030 — September 30, 2031

3. KEY REQUIREMENTS
- Monthly PM inspections per manufacturer specifications
- Emergency repair response: 2-hour on-site within Palo Alto campus, 4-hour for satellite locations
- All technicians must hold EPA Section 608 Universal Certification
- Compliance with VA Directive 7078 (HVAC Maintenance Standards for Healthcare Facilities)
- Background checks required for all on-site personnel (VA PIV card required)

4. EVALUATION CRITERIA
Factor 1: Technical Approach (40%)
Factor 2: Past Performance (30%)
Factor 3: Price (30%)

Best Value Trade-Off methodology

5. ESTIMATED VALUE
Between $1.5 million and $2.5 million annually (full 5-year value: $7.5M-$12.5M)
