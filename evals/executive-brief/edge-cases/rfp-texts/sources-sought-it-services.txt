SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE
Notice Number: IHS-SS-2026-0088

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE ONLY. This is not a solicitation for proposals.

Title: Cloud Hosting, Provisioning, and Managed Services for IHS Health IT Systems
Agency: Indian Health Service (IHS), Office of Information Technology (OIT)
Posted: February 15, 2026

BACKGROUND:
The Indian Health Service provides health care to approximately 2.6 million American Indians and Alaska Natives through a network of 605 hospitals, clinics, and health stations. IHS OIT manages the IT infrastructure supporting the Resource and Patient Management System (RPMS), electronic health records, and telehealth platforms.

IHS is exploring options for migrating existing on-premises health IT workloads to FedRAMP-authorized cloud environments. This sources sought notice is issued to gather information from qualified vendors who can provide:

1. Cloud hosting and infrastructure provisioning (IaaS/PaaS)
2. Managed services including monitoring, patching, and incident response
3. Data migration services from legacy systems
4. HIPAA and FedRAMP High compliance support
5. 24/7 help desk and tier 2/3 technical support

NAICS Code: 541519 — Other Computer Related Services
Small Business Size Standard: $30 million

INFORMATION REQUESTED:
Interested parties should submit a capability statement (10 pages max) addressing:
a) Company profile, relevant certifications, and socioeconomic status
b) Experience with similar healthcare IT cloud migrations
c) FedRAMP authorization status or path to authorization
d) Approach to ensuring HIPAA compliance during migration
e) Proposed partnership or subcontracting arrangements

Responses are requested by March 30, 2026, but late responses will be accepted and reviewed. This notice does not obligate the Government to issue a solicitation or make an award.
