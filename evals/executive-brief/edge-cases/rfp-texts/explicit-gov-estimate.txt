1. BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE

The FBI CJIS Division operates the Next Generation Identification (NGI) system, the nation's largest biometric database serving over 18,000 law enforcement agencies. The current identity verification module requires modernization to incorporate multi-modal biometric matching (fingerprint, facial recognition, iris, and voice) with sub-second response times.

The Government's Independent Cost Estimate (IGE) for this requirement is $47.5 million over a 5-year period (3-year base + 2 option years). Offerors should note this estimate when preparing cost proposals.

2. SCOPE

The contractor shall design, develop, test, deploy, and maintain a Next Generation Identity Verification Platform that:
- Processes 500,000+ identity verification transactions daily
- Achieves 99.95% biometric match accuracy
- Maintains sub-500ms average response time
- Integrates with existing NGI/IAFIS infrastructure
- Supports NIST SP 800-76 and FBI EBTS specifications
- Achieves FedRAMP High + CJIS Security Policy compliance
- Provides 99.99% availability SLA

3. PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE
Base Period: 36 months from award
Option Year 1: 12 months
Option Year 2: 12 months

4. KEY PERSONNEL
