NAICS Code: 541512 — Computer Systems Design Services

THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION. This is a Sources Sought notice to determine industry interest and capability.

1. BACKGROUND

The Bureau of Indian Education (BIE) is conducting market research to identify qualified vendors capable of providing enterprise backup and disaster recovery solutions for approximately 180 school sites across 23 states.

BIE currently manages data across multiple environments. The current insurance policy covering data loss events is valued at $2.5 million annually. Additionally, BIE maintains a $500,000 performance bond with its current infrastructure hosting provider. The annual IT operating budget allocation for storage services is approximately $3.2 million across all programs.

2. SCOPE OF INTEREST

BIE is interested in solutions that provide:
- Automated backup of on-premises and cloud workloads
- Disaster recovery with defined RPO/RTO objectives
- Data deduplication and compression
- Compliance with FISMA Moderate baseline
- Support for Windows Server, Linux, and VMware environments
- Geographic redundancy across at least two data centers

3. CURRENT ENVIRONMENT

BIE currently utilizes Rubrik for backup operations across its primary data center. The Rubrik platform handles approximately 450TB of protected data. Veritas NetBackup is used at 12 remote sites for legacy system backups.

4. REQUESTED INFORMATION

Interested vendors should provide:
a) Company name, address, DUNS number, and business size/type
b) Brief capability statement (no more than 10 pages)
c) Description of similar government deployments
d) Pricing model overview (subscription vs. perpetual licensing)
