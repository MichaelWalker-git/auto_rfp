1. BACKGROUND

The USDA Forest Service Pacific Southwest Region manages over 20 million acres of National Forest land across California. The Forest Inventory and Analysis (FIA) program collects data on tree species, growth, mortality, and forest health metrics across approximately 15,000 permanent sample plots.

The current FIA data platform was developed in 2012 using Oracle Forms and requires modernization to support cloud-native architectures, mobile field data collection, and advanced analytics capabilities.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

The contractor shall:
2.1 Migrate existing FIA database (approximately 2.3TB) from Oracle to PostgreSQL on AWS GovCloud
2.2 Develop a responsive web application for data entry, query, and visualization
2.3 Develop native iOS and Android mobile applications for field data collection with offline capability
2.4 Implement GIS integration with ArcGIS Enterprise for spatial analysis
2.5 Provide data migration services including validation and reconciliation
2.6 Conduct user acceptance testing with FIA field crews
2.7 Provide 12 months of post-deployment support and maintenance

3. PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE

Base Period: 18 months from date of award
Option Period 1: 12 months maintenance and support
Option Period 2: 12 months maintenance and support

Estimated award date: March 2025
