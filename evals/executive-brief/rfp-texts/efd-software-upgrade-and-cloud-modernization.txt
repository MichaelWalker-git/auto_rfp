6. RATIONALE JUSTIFYING USE OF CITED STATUTORY AUTHORITY
The purpose of this Justification is to obtain approval to proceed with a limited source acquisition for services based on items peculiar to
one manufacturer.
The Momentum Financials software suite is the foundational software platform of the Enterprise Funds Distribution (EFD) system. DFAS
requires professional Information Technology services toward upgrading to the current Momentum Financials software, integrating the
updated software to the EFD system, and then migrating the EFD system to a DoD approved Enterprise Commercial Cloud provider. This
requirement is brand name to Momentum Financials. The Momentum Financials is a proprietary software solution which is owned by
CGI Federal (CGI). Since the needs of DFAS require changes to the the Momentum codebase, only a vendor who can alter the
Momentum codebase will suffice to meet this requirement. CGI does not allow other vendors to alter the Momentum software. Only
CGI has this ability.
DFAS intends to sole source this requirement to CGI under its GSA Federal Supply Schedule utilizing FAR 8.4 procedures.
