Haskell Indian Nations University - Rubrik Data Management Solution
Statement of Work (SOW)
OVERVIEW
Haskell Indian Nations University (HINU) seeks to enhance its data management and
security infrastructure by implementing Rubrik's comprehensive data backup and recovery
solution. HINU manages sensitive student, faculty, staff, and administrative data within
Haskell's Microsoft tenant and across the university's KanREN network that requires robust
protection and rapid recovery capabilities. This initiative aligns with Haskell's mission to
provide a secure, innovative, and supportive learning environment for its students, faculty,
and staff. Rubrik's solution will ensure data integrity, security, and availability, safeguarding
HINU's information assets from data loss, cyberattacks, and operational disruptions,
thereby strengthening student success, faculty research, and administrative efficiency, in
support of Haskell's educational and administrative functions.
OBJECTIVE
Haskell Indian Nations University seeks to contract for the successful deployment of
Rubrik's data management solution to:
Enhance data security and integrity across the university;
Provide reliable, efficient and scalable data backup solution and recovery;
Support the university's mission by ensuring continuous access to critical data;
Minimize data loss risks and downtime in case of cyber threats or operational
disruptions;
Streamline and automate backup management to increase IT efficiency and reduce
operational risks;
Align with HINU's strategic goals of maintaining a secure, innovative and resilient IT
infrastructure that supports the university's educational mission.
SCOPE
Rubrik's Data Management Solution's successful implementation will result in the
activation of Rubrik's Premium Support which will provide Haskell with direct and
immediate access to an experienced team of support engineers to help HINU minimize
operational costs and ensure services in the university's mission-critical data management
environment are running optimally. Contractor will create collaboration with Haskell IT to
identify and address technical challenges, improve operational efficiency, and maximize
HINU's investment in Rubrik. Premium support includes these benefits:
Telephone and web support: Rubrik's support professionals will be available by
telephone or web interface on a 24x7 basis. Haskell IT will have access to web-
based knowledge and self-help customer support tools that can help minimize
operational disruption;
Product updates and fixes: To ensure Haskell's IT environment is equipped with the
latest Rubrik features and functionality, Rubrik support engineers will keep Haskell's
IT team apprised of latest software updates and new releases. Any critical product
fixes will also be handled, allowing Haskell IT to maintain a consistent, high level of
productivity;
Advanced hardware replacement: For any hardware failures, Rubrik will ship an
advanced replacement to arrive at Haskell the next business day. Request must be
submitted before 3pm local time to receive next business day service.
TASKS
Contractor will ensure successful installation of Rubrik's Data Management Solution
services and product enablement including topics such as daily administration, SLA
creation, object protection, data recovery options, and cloud archiving. These professional
services solutions will help Haskell IT understand, adopt, leverage, and maintain new
approaches to managing data protection. The following tasks will be undertaken to achieve
the project objectives:
1. Assessment and Planning: Conduct a comprehensive assessment of HINU's
current data infrastructure and develop a detailed implementation plan.
2. Installation and Configuration: Install and configure Rubrik's data management
solution, ensuring seamless integration with existing systems.
3. Data Migration: Migrate existing data to the new system with minimal disruption to
university operations.
4. Testing and Validation: Perform rigorous testing to validate the system's
performance, security, and reliability.
5. Training and Support: Provide training sessions for IT staff, faculty, and other
stakeholders to ensure effective use of the new system.
6. Monitoring and Maintenance: Establish ongoing monitoring and maintenance
protocols to ensure the system's optimal performance.
DELIVERABLES
The project will deliver the following:
Implementation Plan: A detailed plan outlining the steps for deploying Rubrik's
solution.
Installed and Configured System: A fully operational Rubrik data management
system integrated with HINU's existing infrastructure.
Data Migration Report: Documentation of the data migration process and
outcomes.
Testing and Validation Report: Results of the system testing and validation
procedures.
Training Materials: Comprehensive training materials and resources for Haskell IT
staff and end-users.
Maintenance Plan: A plan for ongoing system monitoring and maintenance.
