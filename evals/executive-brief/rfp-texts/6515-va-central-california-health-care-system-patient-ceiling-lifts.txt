Salient Characteristics – Ceiling Lift



Minimum load capacity of 600 lbs. for non-bariatric patient rooms and 1,000 lbs. for bariatric patient rooms.

Handheld remote control to raise/lower patients and move motor along the track.

For motors with a maximum load capacity of 600 pounds, they must have minimum lifting speed of:

1.5 inches per second for a minimum of 440 pounds being lifted

1.3 inches per second for a minimum of 600 pounds being lifted

For motors with a maximum load capacity of 1,000 pounds, they must have minimum lifting speed of:

1.4 inches per second for a minimum of 500 pounds being lifted

1.2 inches per second for a minimum of 1,000 pounds being lifted

Minimum strap length of 98.4 inches to lift a patient up off the ground for motors with maximum load capacity of 600 pounds.

Minimum strap length of 90.6 inches to lift a patient up off the ground for motors with maximum load capacity of 1,000 pounds.

Operates at maximum of 61 dBA.

For motors with a maximum load capacity of 600 pounds, they must have a minimum useful service life of 10-years or 20,000 cycles where a cycle is based on transferring a 600-pound load being raised a minimum of 24 inches, a lateral displacement, and a minimum 24-inch descending action.

For motors with a maximum load capacity of 1,000 pounds, they must have a minimum useful service life of 7-years or 10,000 cycles.

Non-porous components – lifting strap, emergency cord, and spreader bar to enhance infection control properties to be resistant against dirt/contaminants and be easy to clean/disinfect.

LCD interface to provide instant and clear feedback on the lift:

Must indicate battery life, over duty cycle, maintenance required notification, and overweight notifications

Strap with quick release attachment that allows for frontline staff to easily change out spreader bar and use scale without any tools.

Spreader bars with quick release attachment that is compatible with quick release on strap.

Contractor must have all of the following spreader bar options available to meet individual patient needs:

clip style

loop style

2-point and 4-point

powered positioning via remote control

minimum width 24  inches

maximum width 28 inches

non-powered positioning

minimum width 22  inches

Scale with minimum weight capacity of 600 pounds and maximum weight capacity of 1,000 pounds.

Scale that has quick release attachment that is compatible with quick release strap and quick release spreader bar.

Option for powered positioning system.

Emergency braking and lowering system.

Ceiling lift has IP24 protection rating on the lift and IP7 on the handheld remote control, or better.
